AI Architect helps teams turn promising AI ideas into well-designed systems that fit existing products, data flows, and operational constraints.

## Who It Helps

This service is for teams that know what they want to build but need experienced guidance on architecture, integration, reliability, and production readiness.

## What We Do

- Design AI product and internal-tool architectures.
- Map data, model, retrieval, evaluation, and application boundaries.
- Review vendor, model, and infrastructure choices.
- Define production patterns for observability, security, maintainability, and scale.

## Typical Outcomes

- A clear system design your team can build from.
- Better technical tradeoffs before implementation begins.
- Reduced risk around data quality, model behavior, and operations.
- A practical path from prototype to reliable production software.
