AI Advisor helps leaders decide where AI can create real leverage and how to move from scattered ideas to focused action.

## Who It Helps

This service is for founders, executives, and technical leaders who need a clear AI strategy before investing heavily in tools, vendors, prototypes, or hiring.

## What We Do

- Identify AI opportunities with practical business value.
- Prioritize ideas by feasibility, risk, data readiness, and expected impact.
- Shape pilot-to-production roadmaps that your team can execute.
- Help leadership and technical teams align on realistic next steps.

## Typical Outcomes

- A sharper AI strategy tied to business priorities.
- A short list of high-value use cases worth pursuing.
- Clear recommendations for build, buy, partner, or pause decisions.
- A pragmatic roadmap for moving from exploration to delivery.
